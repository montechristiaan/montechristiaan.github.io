About Me

Christiaan Simmons is a veteran of the TV News Industry and beginning Web Developer. He is a husband and the father of three beautiful children. Christiaan started his career in New York and worked as a Producer in Las Vegas, Chicago, and the Twin Cities.

Since the advent of the web and social media, the News Industry has changed and Christiaan is making the leap along with it. He hopes to discover new ways to keep the world informed without sacrificing the integrity of the journalistic spirit.

News isn't all Christiaan is interested in developing however. Since his birth in 1977, Christiaan has watched videogames grow and evolve.. and he would love to be a part of that dynamic industry. Whether thinking of new story ideas or designing new worlds, Christiaan has always had the creative bug and this is the latest media form that he's ready to learn, test, and eventually take in new directions.
